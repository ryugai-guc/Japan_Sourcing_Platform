---

# Title

Catalog First Strategy

---

# Context

Many sourcing websites display thousands of individual products.

Maintaining such a website requires significant effort.

---

# Decision

Catalog becomes the primary marketing material.

Products support catalogs,

instead of catalogs supporting products.

---

# Catalog Types

Brand Catalog

Category Catalog

Season Collection

Exhibition Collection

Special Selection

OEM Collection

---

# Reasons

Catalogs are easier to maintain.

Better presentation.

Suitable for exhibitions.

Supports email marketing.

Suitable for B2B communication.

---

# Business Benefits

Higher Lead conversion.

Professional corporate image.

Easy content management.

Supports future CRM.

---

# Future Expansion

Automatic Catalog Distribution
