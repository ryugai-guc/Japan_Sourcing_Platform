ADR-0003

Version

V1.0

Accepted

Date

2026-06-20

---

# Title

Do Not Implement Customer Portal in Version 1.0

---

# Context

One proposal suggested allowing customers to register and manage their own accounts.

---

# Decision

Version 1.0

will NOT include

Customer Registration

Customer Login

Customer Portal

---

# Reasons

The current customer volume is limited.

Relationship management is mainly through

Email

WeChat

Telephone

Developing a complete portal would increase project scope.

---

# Expected Benefits

Simpler website.

Higher inquiry conversion.

Lower development cost.

Lower maintenance effort.

---

# Future Plan

Customer Portal

may be introduced

after stable customer growth.
