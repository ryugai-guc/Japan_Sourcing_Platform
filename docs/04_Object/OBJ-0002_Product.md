# 1. Purpose

This document defines the Product business object of the Japan Sourcing Platform (JSP).

A Product represents a purchasable item provided by a Supplier. It serves as one of the core Master Data objects within the platform and provides the foundation for Catalogs, Inquiries, Quotations, Purchase Orders, and future business processes.

---

# 2. Business Purpose

The Product object is designed to:

- Maintain standardized product master information.
- Organize supplier products consistently.
- Support multilingual product information.
- Provide searchable product data.
- Support catalog publication.
- Support customer inquiries.
- Support quotation generation.
- Support future ERP integration.

Product itself does not represent a commercial transaction.

---

# 3. Business Rules

## BR-001

Every Product belongs to one Supplier.

Relationship:

Supplier (1) → Product (N)

---

## BR-002

Each Product shall have one unique System Product ID.

Example:

PRD-000001

The System Product ID is generated by the system and shall never be modified.

---

## BR-003

Supplier Product Code is optional.

It represents the product code provided by the supplier.

Example:

MCX-A501

Supplier Product Code is not the database primary key.

---

## BR-004

Product Name

Required:

- Japanese Name

Optional:

- English Name
- Chinese Name

---

## BR-005

Product may exist before complete information is collected.

Missing information such as Supplier Product Code, images, specifications, or category may be added later.

---

## BR-006

A Product may have multiple images.

One image shall be designated as the Primary Image.

---

## BR-007

A Product belongs to one Category.

Category assignment is optional during initial creation.

---

## BR-008

A Product may or may not belong to a Brand.

Brand assignment is optional.

---

## BR-009

Different Products may have the same Product Name.

Product uniqueness is determined by System Product ID.

---

## BR-010

Product records the source of information.

Examples:

- Trade Show
- Supplier Website
- Email
- Catalog PDF
- WeChat

---

## BR-011

Product maintains audit information.

Including:

- Created Time
- Updated Time
- Updated By

---

## BR-012

Product does not maintain commercial prices.

Prices belong to Quotation.

# 8. Design Principles

## Identity Separation

System Product ID and Supplier Product Code are independent.

The database shall always use System Product ID.

---

## Collect First

Product information may be incomplete during initial collection.

Information shall be improved gradually.

---

## Master Data

Product is Master Data.

Business transactions shall not modify Product identity.

---

## Price Separation

Commercial prices belong to Quotation rather than Product.
