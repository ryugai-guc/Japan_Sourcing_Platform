# 1. Purpose

This document defines the Category business object of the Japan Sourcing Platform (JSP).

Category provides a standardized classification system for organizing Products throughout the platform.

Category is maintained by JSP and is independent of Supplier-defined categories.

---

# 2. Business Purpose

The Category object is designed to:

* Organize Products consistently.
* Provide website navigation.
* Support product searching and filtering.
* Support Catalog generation.
* Support future SEO optimization.
* Support AI product recommendation.

Category represents JSP's own classification standard.

---

# 3. Business Rules

## BR-001

Category is maintained by JSP.

Supplier category definitions shall not directly determine JSP categories.

---

## BR-002

Category supports hierarchical structure.

Maximum hierarchy depth:

Level 1

↓

Level 2

↓

Level 3

---

## BR-003

Every Product belongs to one Category.

Category assignment is optional during initial Product creation.

---

## BR-004

Root Categories may exist without a parent.

Parent Category is optional.

---

## BR-005

Category supports display ordering.

Display Order determines navigation menu sequence and presentation order.

---

## BR-006

Category Image is optional.

Images may be used for website navigation or promotional pages.

---

## BR-007

Category Description is optional.

Description may be used for SEO and business explanation.

---

## BR-008

Every Category shall have lifecycle status.

Examples:

* Active
* Inactive
* Archived

---

## BR-009

Physical deletion is prohibited.

Category records shall be managed through lifecycle status.

---

## BR-010

Category Name

Required:

* Japanese Name

Optional:

* English Name
* Chinese Name

---

## BR-011

Supplier Categories shall be mapped to JSP Categories through a mapping relationship.

Supplier category definitions remain unchanged.

JSP maintains its own standardized classification.

---

## BR-012

Every Category shall have one unique System Category ID.

Example:

CAT-000001

The System Category ID is generated by the system and shall never be modified.

# 8. Design Principles

## Platform-Owned Classification

Category belongs to JSP.

Supplier classification is preserved through mapping.

---

## Hierarchical Structure

Categories are organized in a three-level tree structure.

---

## Navigation-Oriented

Category supports website navigation, search filtering, and Catalog organization.

---

## Logical Delete

Physical deletion is prohibited.

Lifecycle status shall be used for retirement.

---

## Multilingual Support

Japanese Name is mandatory.

English and Chinese names are optional.
