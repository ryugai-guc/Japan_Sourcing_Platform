# 1. Purpose

This document defines the Lead business object of the Japan Sourcing Platform (JSP).

A Lead represents a potential business opportunity before a formal Inquiry is established.

Lead is the starting point of the CRM process.

---

# 2. Business Purpose

The Lead object is designed to:

- Capture potential business opportunities.
- Record initial customer information.
- Support sales follow-up.
- Qualify customer intentions.
- Convert qualified opportunities into Inquiries.

Lead is not a Customer, Inquiry, or Order.

---

# 3. Business Trigger

A Lead may be created from:

- Website Contact Form
- Website Inquiry Form
- Email
- Phone Call
- Exhibition / Trade Show
- Referral
- Manual Entry

---

# 4. Business Rules

## BR-001

Every Lead shall have one unique System Lead ID.

Example:

LD-000001

The System Lead ID is generated by the system and shall never be modified.

---

## BR-002

Contact Person is required.

Company Name is optional.

---

## BR-003

The following contact fields shall be available:

- Email
- Phone
- Fax

Each field may be empty.

However, at least one contact method shall be provided.

---

## BR-004

Every Lead shall have one Sales Owner.

Default Owner = Created By.

Ownership may be reassigned later.

---

## BR-005

Lead Source is required.

Supported sources include:

- Website
- Email
- Phone
- Exhibition
- Referral
- Manual

---

## BR-006

A Lead may exist without any Product.

Product selection is optional during the Lead stage.

---

## BR-007

Expected Interest Category is optional.

It represents the customer's general business interest.

It is not a Product assignment.

---

## BR-008

A Lead may be converted into one or more Inquiries.

Relationship:

Lead (1)

↓

Inquiry (N)

Conversion is one-way.

---

## BR-009

After conversion, the Lead remains in the system.

Status changes to **Converted**.

Physical deletion is prohibited.

---

## BR-010

Every Lead shall have lifecycle status.

Suggested values:

- New
- Contacted
- Qualified
- Converted
- Closed

---

## BR-011

Lead follow-up activities are managed independently through the Activity object.

Lead does not directly maintain activity history.

---

## BR-012

Next Follow-up Date is supported.

This field is used to schedule future customer communication.

# 9. Design Principles

## Opportunity-Oriented

Lead represents a business opportunity before a formal Inquiry.

---

## Contact-First

A Lead shall always contain a valid contact person and at least one contact method.

---

## Owner Responsibility

Every Lead has exactly one responsible Sales Owner.

---

## Logical Delete

Physical deletion is prohibited.

Lifecycle status shall be used instead.

---

## Activity Separation

Business activities are maintained independently from the Lead object.
