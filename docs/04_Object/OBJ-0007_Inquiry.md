# 1. Purpose

This document defines the Inquiry business object of the Japan Sourcing Platform (JSP).

An Inquiry represents a customer's purchasing request and serves as the entry point of the sourcing process.

Inquiry connects Customer Demand with Product Supply.

---

# 2. Business Purpose

The Inquiry object is designed to:

- Record customer purchasing requests.
- Manage requested products.
- Support supplier sourcing.
- Generate quotations.
- Track inquiry lifecycle.

Inquiry is the core business object of JSP.

---

# 3. Business Trigger

An Inquiry may be created from:

- Lead Conversion
- Website Inquiry
- Email
- Phone
- Manual Entry

---

# 4. Business Rules

## BR-001

Every Inquiry shall have one unique System Inquiry ID.

Example:

INQ-000001

The System Inquiry ID is generated by the system and shall never be modified.

---

## BR-002

An Inquiry may be created with or without a Lead.

Lead relationship is optional.

---

## BR-003

Every Inquiry shall have one Sales Owner.

Default Owner = Created By.

Ownership may be reassigned.

---

## BR-004

Customer Contact Person is required.

Customer Company is optional.

---

## BR-005

Inquiry Source is required.

Examples:

- Lead
- Website
- Email
- Phone
- Exhibition
- Manual

---

## BR-006

Priority is required.

Suggested values:

- High
- Medium
- Low

---

## BR-007

Requested Due Date is supported.

---

## BR-008

An Inquiry supports multiple attachments.

Examples:

- PDF
- Excel
- Image
- Drawing

---

## BR-009

One Inquiry may generate multiple Quotations.

Relationship:

Inquiry (1)

↓

Quotation (N)

---

## BR-010

Every Inquiry shall have lifecycle status.

Suggested values:

- New
- Under Review
- Sourcing
- Quoting
- Quoted
- Closed

---

## BR-011

Physical deletion is prohibited.

Lifecycle status shall be used.

---

# 5. Inquiry Item

Every Inquiry contains one or more Inquiry Items.

Inquiry Item represents one requested product.

Each Inquiry Item may optionally reference an existing Product.

Product association is optional during the early sourcing stage.

Each Inquiry Item supports:

- Product
- Requested Description
- Requested Quantity
- Unit
- Remark

# 10. Design Principles

## Inquiry-Driven

Inquiry is the entry point of every sourcing project.

---

## Header / Line Structure

Inquiry manages Inquiry Items.

Items represent individual requested products.

---

## Flexible Product Matching

Inquiry Items may exist before Products are identified.

---

## Owner Responsibility

Every Inquiry has one Sales Owner.

---

## Logical Delete

Physical deletion is prohibited.
