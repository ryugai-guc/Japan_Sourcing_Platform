# 1. Purpose

This document defines the Brand business object of the Japan Sourcing Platform (JSP).

A Brand represents the commercial identity under which products are marketed.

Brand is an independent Master Data object and is not owned by any Supplier.

---

# 2. Business Purpose

The Brand object is designed to:

- Standardize brand information.
- Support product classification.
- Support product searching and filtering.
- Support catalog presentation.
- Support future business expansion.

Brand does not represent a Supplier or Manufacturer.

---

# 3. Business Rules

## BR-001

Brand is an independent Master Data object.

It shall not belong to any specific Supplier.

---

## BR-002

A Supplier may be associated with multiple Brands.

A Brand may also be associated with multiple Suppliers.

Relationship:

Supplier (N) ↔ Brand (N)

---

## BR-003

A Product may or may not belong to a Brand.

Brand assignment is optional.

---

## BR-004

Every Brand shall have one unique System Brand ID.

Example:

BRD-000001

The Brand ID is generated by the system and shall never be modified.

---

## BR-005

Brand Name

Required:

- Japanese Name

Optional:

- English Name
- Chinese Name

---

## BR-006

Logo is optional.

If available, it may be displayed in Catalogs and Product pages.

---

## BR-007

Official Website is optional.

---

## BR-008

Brand Description is optional.

It may include:

- Brand introduction
- Brand philosophy
- Product characteristics

---

## BR-009

Different Brands may have the same display name.

Brand uniqueness is determined by System Brand ID.

---

## BR-010

Brand maintains audit information.

Including:

- Created Time
- Updated Time
- Updated By

---

## BR-011

Physical deletion is prohibited.

Brand records shall be managed through lifecycle status.

# 8. Design Principles

## Independent Master Data

Brand is an independent business object.

It is not a Supplier attribute.

---

## Lightweight Design

Brand stores only brand-level information.

Commercial and transaction data belong to other business objects.

---

## Logical Delete

Brand records shall never be physically deleted.

Lifecycle status shall be used instead.

---

## Multilingual Support

Japanese Name is mandatory.

English and Chinese names are optional.
