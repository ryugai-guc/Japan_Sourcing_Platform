# 1. Purpose

This document defines the Quotation business object of the Japan Sourcing Platform (JSP).

A Quotation is the official commercial offer issued by JSP to a customer.

Quotation is generated based on an Inquiry and represents JSP's commercial commitment to the customer.

---

# 2. Business Purpose

The Quotation object is designed to:

- Respond to customer inquiries.
- Provide official commercial pricing.
- Define commercial terms.
- Support quotation revisions.
- Serve as the basis for Purchase Orders.

Quotation is an official business document.

---

# 3. Business Trigger

A Quotation may be created from:

- Approved Inquiry
- Inquiry Revision
- Manual Sales Operation

---

# 4. Business Rules

## BR-001

Every Quotation shall have one unique System Quotation ID.

Example:

QT-000001

The Quotation ID is generated by the system and shall never be modified.

---

## BR-002

Every Quotation shall reference exactly one Inquiry.

Relationship:

Inquiry (1)

↓

Quotation (N)

---

## BR-003

Every Quotation shall reference exactly one Supplier.

A Quotation shall never contain products from multiple Suppliers.

If multiple Suppliers participate in one Inquiry, separate Quotations shall be created.

---

## BR-004

One Inquiry may generate multiple Quotations.

Examples:

- Different Suppliers
- Revised Quotations
- Partial Quotations

---

## BR-005

Partial quotation is supported.

A Supplier may quote only selected Inquiry Items.

---

## BR-006

Quotation Version is mandatory.

Version starts from Version 1.

Each revision generates a new Version.

Approved or Issued versions shall never be modified.

Historical versions remain read-only.

---

## BR-007

Quotation currency is fixed to JPY.

Multiple currencies are not supported.

---

## BR-008

Every Quotation shall define:

- Valid Until Date

Expired quotations shall not be accepted.

---

## BR-009

Payment Terms shall be specified.

Examples:

- T/T
- L/C
- Cash

---

## BR-010

Incoterms shall be specified.

Examples:

- EXW
- FOB
- CIF
- DDP

---

## BR-011

Every quotation shall have one Sales Owner.

Field:

The Sales Owner is responsible for:

Preparing quotation
Issuing quotation
Customer communication
Quotation revision
Business follow-up

Ownership may be reassigned before quotation issuance.

For issued quotations, the Sales Owner recorded on the quotation becomes part of the historical business record.

---

## BR-012

Every Quotation shall have Lifecycle Status.

Suggested values:

- Draft
- Issued
- Accepted
- Rejected
- Expired
- Superseded

---

## BR-013

Physical deletion is prohibited.

Only Lifecycle Status may change.

Business content becomes immutable after Issue.

---

# 5. Quotation Item

Each Quotation contains one or more Quotation Items.

Each Quotation Item represents one quoted product.

Quotation Item supports:

- Product
- Unit Price (JPY)
- MOQ
- Lead Time
- Remark

Quotation Item is derived from Inquiry Item.

# 10. Design Principles

## Official Commercial Document

Quotation represents JSP's formal commercial offer.

---

## Single Supplier Principle

One Quotation corresponds to one Supplier only.

---

## Header / Line Structure

Quotation manages one or more Quotation Items.

---

## Immutable Business Record

Once Issued:

- Business content cannot be modified.
- New business changes require a new Version.
- Historical versions remain read-only.

Reference:

ADR-0005 Immutable Business Records

---

## JPY Only

All quotations use Japanese Yen (JPY).

Multi-currency quotations are not supported.

---

## Version-Based History

Business history is preserved through quotation versions.

Previous versions remain permanently available.
