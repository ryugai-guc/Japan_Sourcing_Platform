# 1 Purpose

This document defines the business model of Japan Sourcing Platform (JSP).

The platform is designed to support B2B sourcing between Japanese suppliers and overseas buyers.

---

# 2 Business Position

JSP is a Japan Product Discovery and Sourcing Platform.

It is not an online shopping mall.

Its primary objective is to generate qualified business inquiries and convert them into long-term trading relationships.
