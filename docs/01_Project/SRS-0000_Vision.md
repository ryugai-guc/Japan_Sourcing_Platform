# 1 Purpose

This document defines the vision of Japan Sourcing Platform (JSP).

It serves as the highest-level guideline for all future business and software design decisions.

---

# 2 Vision

Help overseas customers continuously discover and source excellent Japanese products.

JSP is not simply selling products.

JSP provides the capability to discover valuable products in Japan.

---

# 3 Mission

Build a trusted sourcing platform connecting Japanese suppliers with overseas buyers.

Reduce communication costs.

Improve sourcing efficiency.

Create long-term business relationships.

---

# 4 Business Value

For Customers

- Discover new Japanese products
- Reduce sourcing costs
- Access trusted suppliers
- Receive professional sourcing support

For Suppliers

- Increase overseas exposure
- Expand export opportunities
- Receive qualified inquiries

For JSP

- Build long-term customer relationships
- Improve sourcing efficiency
- Create sustainable trading business

---

# 5 Core Principles

Customer First

Professional Sourcing

Long-term Cooperation

Quality before Quantity

Trust before Transaction

---

# 6 Success Criteria

Continuous Lead Generation

High Inquiry Conversion

Stable Supplier Network

Long-term Customer Retention

Scalable Digital Platform

---

# 7 Future Vision

Become one of the most trusted Japan sourcing platforms serving overseas markets.
