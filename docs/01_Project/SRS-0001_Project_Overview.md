# 1 Project Name

Japan Sourcing Platform

# 2 Background

The company has been operating in Japan for more than 12 years.

The business has accumulated supplier resources through exhibitions and business activities.

To improve sourcing efficiency and customer acquisition, a digital platform is required.

---

# 3 Project Goal

Build an official sourcing platform that enables overseas customers to:

- Discover Japanese products
- Submit sourcing inquiries
- Receive quotations
- Establish long-term cooperation

---

# 4 Business Scope

- Quote management
- Supplier management
- Lead management

Excluded
