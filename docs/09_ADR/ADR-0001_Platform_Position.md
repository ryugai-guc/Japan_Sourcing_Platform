# Title

Japan Sourcing Platform Positioning

---

# Context

At the beginning of the project, several platform models were considered.

Option A

Online Shopping Mall

Option B

Marketplace

Option C

Japan Sourcing Platform

---

# Decision

The platform will be positioned as a

Japan Product Discovery Platform

and

Japan Sourcing Service Platform.

It will not become a traditional online shopping website.

---

# Reasons

The company operates as a Japanese trading company.

Business is mainly B2B.

Prices vary depending on

- Quantity
- Exchange Rate
- Shipping
- Procurement Cost

Manual quotation is more suitable.

---

# Consequences

Advantages

Professional image.

Suitable for long-term cooperation.

Easy supplier management.

Flexible pricing.

Disadvantages

No instant purchase.

Requires manual quotation.

Longer sales cycle.

---

# Alternatives Considered

Shopping Mall

Rejected.

Marketplace

Rejected.
