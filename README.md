# Japan Sourcing Platform (JSP)

Version: V1.0
Status: Approved

---

## Introduction

Japan Sourcing Platform (JSP) is a digital sourcing platform operated by a Japanese trading company.

The platform is designed to help overseas customers discover high-quality Japanese products and submit sourcing inquiries through a simple and efficient process.

Unlike traditional e-commerce websites, JSP focuses on product discovery, sourcing services and business communication instead of direct online purchasing.

---

## Business Position

JSP is NOT:

- Online Shopping Mall
- Marketplace
- B2C E-commerce

JSP IS:

- Japan Product Discovery Platform
- Japan Sourcing Service
- B2B Trading Platform
- Lead Generation Platform
- CRM-enabled Trading Website
